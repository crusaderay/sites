Memo Sticky

Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Privacy Overview

This privacy policy describes how Memo Sticky ("Application", "we", "our") handles information when you use the app. Memo Sticky is designed around local note storage and device-side protection, with limited third-party analytics and advertising services used to support the product.

At a Glance

📝

No Direct Note Collection

We do not directly collect note content, scanned documents, voice recordings, transcription text, or other personal note data from you.

📱

Stored on Your Device

Notes and preferences are stored locally on your device, with support for secure storage and biometric protection.

🔐

Permission-Based Features

Camera, microphone, speech recognition, and Face ID or Touch ID are only used after you grant permission for those features.

Information We Do Not Collect

Memo Sticky is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or process the following data directly:

  • Your IP address or device identifiers
  • Personal contact information
  • Note content or text data
  • Voice recordings or transcription content
  • Scanned documents or image content
  • Any other personal identification information

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to provide app functionality and improvements. These services may process limited anonymous data.

📈

Firebase Analytics

Purpose: Crash reporting, performance monitoring, and usage analytics.

Data: Anonymous app usage patterns, crash reports, and device performance metrics.

Retention: Up to 26 months according to Google policy.

📣

Google Mobile Ads

Purpose: Advertising display and ad-effectiveness measurement.

Data: Anonymous advertising identifiers and ad interaction data.

Retention: Managed according to Google's advertising policies.

Storage and Security

💾

Local Data Storage

All note data and user preferences are stored locally on your device using secure operating-system storage mechanisms.

We use encrypted local storage, secure keychain or keystore integration, no cloud sync or remote backup, and optional Face ID or Touch ID protection for important notes.

⏳

Retention

Local data: Retained until you delete notes or uninstall the app.

Firebase data: Automatically deleted after 26 months.

Ad data: Managed according to Google's retention policies.

Permissions and Controls

🎤

Device Permissions

Camera permission supports document scanning, microphone and speech recognition support voice-to-text, and Face ID or Touch ID protects note access.

All permissions are used only after your explicit authorization and solely for the related feature.

🛠️

Your Rights and Choices

You can view, edit, or delete saved notes, clear app data through device settings, uninstall the app to remove local data, and manage personalized ads through device settings.

Additional Notes

🌍

International Transfer

Firebase and Google Ads may transfer anonymous data to servers in various countries, including the United States, under Google's privacy and contractual safeguards.

👶

Children's Privacy

Memo Sticky is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate steps.

🌐

External Services

Third-party websites or services linked from the app have their own privacy practices, which should be reviewed separately.

⚖️

Legal Compliance and Updates

This policy is intended to align with GDPR, CCPA, COPPA, and the Personal Information Protection Law of the People's Republic of China. We may revise it as practices or laws change.

Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy or our data processing practices, please contact us. We strive to respond to all inquiries within 30 days.
