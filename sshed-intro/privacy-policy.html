Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Privacy Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how SSHed ("the Application", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application SSHed. The app is designed around local storage for connection data, with limited third-party analytics and advertising services used for app improvement and monetization.

At a Glance

🔒

No Direct Personal Collection

SSHed does not collect SSH credentials, terminal content, personal contact information, or other personally identifiable information directly from you.

💾

Local Device Storage

Saved connections, credentials, and preferences are stored on your device using secure operating-system storage mechanisms.

📊

Limited Third-Party Data

Firebase Analytics and Google Mobile Ads may process limited anonymized data under their own policies.

Information We Do Not Collect

SSHed is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or process the following categories of user data directly:

  • Your IP address or device identifiers
  • Personal contact information
  • SSH connection details or credentials
  • Terminal session content or commands
  • Any other personally identifiable information

Third-Party Services

While we do not collect personal data directly, we use third-party services for application functionality and improvement. These services may collect limited anonymized data.

📈

Firebase Analytics

Purpose: Crash reporting, performance monitoring, and usage analytics.

Data: Anonymized app usage patterns, crash reports, and device performance metrics.

Retention: Up to 26 months according to Google's policy.

📣

Google Mobile Ads

Purpose: Advertising display and ad-performance measurement.

Data: Anonymized advertising identifiers and ad interaction data.

Retention: Managed according to Google's advertising policies.

Storage and Retention

🗄️

Local Data Storage

All SSH connection data, including saved credentials and preferences, is stored exclusively on your local device.

We use encrypted local storage, secure keychain or keystore integration, and do not provide cloud synchronization or remote backup of connection data.

⏳

Retention

Local data: Retained until you manually delete connections or uninstall the app.

Firebase data: Automatically deleted after 26 months.

Ad data: Retained according to Google's policies.

Your Rights and Choices

🛠️

Local Data Management

You can view, edit, or delete saved connections within the app, clear app data through device settings, or uninstall the app to remove local data.

⚙️

Third-Party Controls

You can opt out of personalized ads, reset your advertising identifier, and use any future analytics controls that may be introduced.

Additional Notes

🌍

International Transfers

Firebase and Google Ads may transfer anonymized data to servers in various countries, including the United States, under Google's privacy and contractual safeguards.

👶

Children's Privacy

SSHed is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can address the issue.

🍪

Cookies and External Services

The app itself does not use cookies, but advertising services may rely on similar identifiers. External websites and services linked from the app have their own privacy practices.

⚖️

Legal Compliance and Updates

This policy is intended to align with GDPR, CCPA, and COPPA. We may update it periodically and will revise the last-updated date when material changes are made.

Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 30 days.
