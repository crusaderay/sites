About Deskward

Deskward is a work hours tracker built for people who want to know how much time they actually spent working, not just how long they stayed at a desk. It separates active work time from breaks, supports both office and WFH modes, and keeps your timer visible through an always-on charging screensaver and Live Activity on the Lock Screen.

Key Features

⏱️

Real Work Time Tracking

Track the minutes you were actually working and keep breaks separate, so your total reflects real focus time instead of vague attendance.

🏢

Office and WFH Modes

Switch between working at the office and working from home while keeping one consistent record of your day.

📺

Screensaver and Live Activity

When your phone is charging, Deskward can stay on as an OLED-friendly screensaver. Unplug it and your timer continues on the Lock Screen.

✅

To-Dos That Match Your Work Mode

Create daily and recurring tasks, then show the right to-dos for office days, WFH days, or every workday.

📝

Edit History and Recover Records

Review past sessions, fix missed pauses, and restore deleted records from the trash when you need to clean up your timeline.

📊

Overtime and Weekly Insights

See total hours, pause time, office vs WFH distribution, and overtime based on your own schedule templates instead of a generic nine-to-five.
