About Portable Hole

Portable Hole is your personal digital sanctuary - a secure local storage app that lets you collect, organize, and access important information completely offline. Independent from social media platforms, it provides a dedicated space on your phone to store valuable content that truly belongs to you.

Key Features

  • Complete Privacy & Control: 100% local storage - your data never leaves your device, ensuring complete privacy and independence.
  • Effortless Content Collection: Save web links, add custom descriptions, and attach images or videos to create rich, organized entries.
  • Smart Organization System: Use custom tags to categorize your content and find what you need with powerful search functionality.
  • Offline Independence: Works completely offline without requiring accounts, cloud sync, or dependence on social media platforms.
  • Cross-Platform Availability: Available on iOS and Android, built with Flutter for a smooth, responsive user experience.
  • Clean & Intuitive Design: Beautiful, minimalist interface designed for daily use with no ads, tracking, or data collection.
