Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Privacy Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Portable Hole ("the Application", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the app. Portable Hole is built as a local-first digital vault, so the core experience is designed to keep your saved content on your own device.

At a Glance

🔒

No Direct Content Collection

We do not directly collect your saved links, notes, tags, media attachments, or other personally identifiable content.

📦

Local-First Storage

Your collected content and preferences are stored on your device rather than being synced to our own cloud service.

📊

Limited Service Providers

Firebase Analytics and Google Mobile Ads may process limited anonymized data under their own policies.

Information We Do Not Collect

Portable Hole is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or process the following types of data directly:

  • Your IP address or device identifiers
  • Personal contact information
  • Your saved links, descriptions, or content
  • Images, videos, or attachments you store
  • Tags or organizational data you create
  • Any other personally identifiable information

Third-Party Services

While we do not collect personal data directly, we rely on third-party services for app improvement and monetization. These services may process limited anonymized data.

📈

Firebase Analytics

Purpose: Crash reporting, performance monitoring, and usage analytics.

Data: Anonymized app usage patterns, crash reports, and device performance metrics.

Retention: Up to 26 months according to Google's policy.

📣

Google Mobile Ads

Purpose: Display advertisements and measure ad performance.

Data: Anonymized advertising identifiers and ad interaction data.

Retention: Managed according to Google's advertising policies.

Storage and Retention

💾

Local Storage

Saved links, descriptions, images, videos, tags, and preferences are stored on your device using secure local database and file-system storage.

We do not provide cloud synchronization or remote backup of your personal content.

⏳

Retention

Local data: Retained until you delete entries or uninstall the app.

Firebase data: Automatically deleted after 26 months.

Ad data: Managed according to Google's retention policies.

Your Rights and Choices

🛠️

Local Data Control

You can view, edit, or delete saved entries within the app, clear app data through device settings, or uninstall the app to remove all local content.

⚙️

Advertising and Analytics Controls

You can opt out of personalized ads, reset your advertising identifier, and use any analytics controls that may be introduced in future versions.

Additional Notes

🌍

International Transfers

Third-party services may transfer anonymized data to servers in various countries, including the United States, under Google's privacy and contractual safeguards.

👨‍👩‍👧

Children's Privacy

Portable Hole can be used by all ages because it does not directly collect personal information, though parental guidance is recommended for younger users.

🍪

Cookies and Similar Technologies

The app does not use cookies directly, but advertising services may use similar identifiers that can be managed through device settings.

⚖️

Compliance and Updates

This policy is intended to align with GDPR, CCPA, and COPPA. We may update it as practices or laws change, and the last-updated date will be revised accordingly.

Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 30 days.
